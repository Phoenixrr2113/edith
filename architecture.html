Default Schedule

morning-brief — 8:03 AM — Full context: calendar, email, Cognee, meeting prep
midday-check — 12:07 PM — Light: catch changes, prep afternoon
evening-wrap — 4:53 PM — Day review, tomorrow prep, Cognee store
check-reminders — Every 5 min — Check SQLite reminders table
proactive-check — Every 10 min — Screen context + triggers (local only)
weekly-review — Sunday 5 PM — GTD review, Google Doc
monthly-review — 1st of month 9:30 AM — Life scorecard
quarterly-review — 1st of Jan/Apr/Jul/Oct 10 AM — Strategic review
⚠ COST: check-reminders fires 288x/day at $0.10 each = $29/day for a task that found 1 reminder in 48 hours. No code-level pre-check before LLM dispatch.

⚠ TIMEZONE: Quiet hours use UTC, not local time. Cloud mode fires during evening.

⚠ LOST TASKS: skipIfBusy=true means window tasks (morning-brief) can be permanently skipped if dispatch is busy. No retry.

Key Differences from Current

1. No busy flag — inputs always accepted, never blocked
2. Self-scheduling — Edith creates her own follow-up tasks
3. Persistent state — context accumulates between runs, nothing forgotten
4. Parallel workers — multiple things happen at once
5. Actions over reports — briefs trigger work, not just summaries
6. Signal-driven — reacts to changes, not just timers
